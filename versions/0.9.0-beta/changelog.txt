## Changes Made
- With the update of *Ares-HUD*, the issue where the *Simple Voice Chat* icons were not appearing has been fixed.  
- With the update of *EpicFight Better Lock-on*, the enemy lock-on system has been improved. The camera now works in a more dynamic way.  
- The interface of the *main menu* and the *pause menu* has been updated.  
- A *changelog menu* has been added. When the modpack is launched, it will notify players whenever a new update for the modpack is available.  
- General performance improvements.
